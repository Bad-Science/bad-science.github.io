Hello World, And All Who Inhabit It

Hello, World!

I’m Ada, and this is my blog!

A little about me: I’m a software developer, teacher, maker, tinkerer, open source evangelist, and lots of other things! I’m currently a senior coordinator at RCOS, the Rensselaer Center for Open Source, which is where I spend most of my time. I love teaching and learning from my peers, developing curriculum, giving talks, and maintaining my open source project, Yacs. I’m also involved with MOSSN, the Mozilla Open Source Student Network, where I work with and advise other campus clubs from around the country! I’m graduating college very soon, so soon I’ll have a lot more time to focus on the things I love!

I also love writing and sharing, but I haven’t been doing enough of it! So I made this blog to write about what I’m working on, post guides, opinions, my favorite one-liners, and whatever else I can think of! So check back soon for some great content!

- Ada
